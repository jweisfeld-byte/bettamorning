The "Rosé All Summer" Problem Nobody Names

How To Make It Iced In 60 Seconds

Hot cacao is a winter thing. The iced version is even easier, and it's the one everyone's actually reaching for right now.

The Iced Cacao Summer Ritual

Cold, chocolatey, and prettier than the drink you're replacing.

  1. Drop one cacao heart into a mug with a small splash of hot water and froth for a few seconds until it melts into a smooth base.
  2. Fill a tall glass with ice and your milk of choice, oat and almond both work beautifully.
  3. Pour the cacao over the top and stir. Finish with a little maple and a pinch of flaky salt to make it taste like dessert.
  4. Sip it slowly, in a real glass, on the porch. That part matters more than you'd think.
